Our Facility

See everything we have to offer

Free Weight Room

Our free weight area is designed to cater to individuals at every fitness level, from beginners to seasoned athletes. We boast an extensive range of dumbbells, ranging from a lightweight 5 lbs to a hefty 140 lbs, ensuring you have the perfect tools to challenge and sculpt your muscles. In addition to our wide array of dumbbells, we offer well-equipped squat racks, benches, and a variety of other essential strength training equipment to help you achieve your fitness goals.

Accessory Room

In our accessory room you'll discover a selection of cable machines designed to target specific muscle groups with precision and finesse. From the chest fly to the quad extension and chest press, our state-of-the-art equipment offers a full spectrum of exercises to help you isolate and strengthen key muscle areas.

Cardio Room

This dedicated space is equipped with a variety of cutting-edge machines, including treadmills, stationary bikes, StairMasters, and ellipticals, designed to meet your cardio workout needs.

Posing Room

Here, you'll find an expanse of wall-to-wall mirrors, providing the perfect environment for athletes and fitness enthusiasts to fine-tune their posture and evaluate their technique. Our room is equipped with various aids, such as box jumps, resistance bands, and a turf area, offering a wide array of tools for dynamic stretching, mobility training, and agility work.

Sauna

Our saunas offer a space for you to unwind, detoxify, and soothe your body and mind. With carefully controlled temperatures and a welcoming ambiance
